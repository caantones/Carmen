Coffee Corner

Coffee is a beverage brewed from the roasted and ground seeds of the tropical evergreen coffee plant. Coffee is one of the three most popular beverages in the world, and it is one of the most profitable international commodities.


Arabica is the most popular type of coffee, hands down. Depending on who you ask, many coffee enthusiasts prefer using Arabica beans due to its taste.
Arabica is the most popular, Robusta is a cheaper and stronger type of coffee bean. Because of its bitter flavor, you’ll typically see Robusta used for espresso drinks and in instant coffee mixes.
